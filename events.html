Together We Rise

Coparenting to Raise Joyful Humans

We Rise Together as Coparents to Raise Happy People For the benefit of raising healthy children, this group is for single parents and their co-parents who want to strengthen, develop, and improve their family relationships. In order to foster the development of the co-parenting relationship dynamic, participants will learn how to interact with their co-parent in a courteous and possibly even friendly manner.

  • Practice listening and hearing your fellow co-parent
  • Establishing and respecting boundaries
  • Improving skills at managing individual trauma and histories
  • Improved awareness of what triggers the other co-parent

When: Every Wednesday, January 12 — March 2, 2025
Time: 6:30 PM — 8:00 PM
Location: Community Wellness Center Hall
Fee: AED 50 per session

Healthy Choices, Healthy Lives

Nutrition Workshops

The "Nutrition Workshops" are designed to educate participants on healthy eating habits, meal planning, and practical cooking skills. Led by professional nutritionists, these interactive sessions aim to empower individuals and families to make informed choices for a balanced diet.

  • Learn to read nutrition labels and plan balanced meals.
  • Demonstrations of quick, healthy recipes for busy lifestyles.
  • Tips for managing dietary restrictions (vegan, gluten-free, etc.).

When: Monthly on the 2nd Wednesday
Time: 6:00 PM — 8:00 PM
Location: Community Wellness Center Kitchen.
Fee: AED 30 per session (includes recipe handouts and samples).
